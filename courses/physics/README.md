# Introduction to Physics - Course Overview

**Difficulty:** Beginner  
**Total Lessons:** 10  
**Target Audience:** High school students with no prior physics knowledge

---

## Course Structure

| Lesson | Topic | Key Concepts |
|--------|-------|--------------|
| 1 | What Is Physics? | Matter, Energy, Motion, Forces |
| 2 | Measurement & Units | SI System, Prefixes, Tools |
| 3 | Motion & Rest | Reference Frame, Distance vs Displacement |
| 4 | Speed & Velocity | Speed Formula, Direction |
| 5 | Acceleration | a = (v-u)/t, Positive/Negative |
| 6 | Force | Newton, Types of Forces, Net Force |
| 7 | Newton's Laws | Inertia, F=ma, Action-Reaction |
| 8 | Work, Energy & Power | W=Fd, KE, PE, Conservation |
| 9 | Heat & Temperature | Transfer Methods, Scales |
| 10 | Light & Sound | Waves, Properties, Comparison |
